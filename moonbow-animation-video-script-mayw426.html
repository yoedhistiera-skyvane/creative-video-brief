Moonbow · Complete Calm Trio · Creative Production Brief

Complete Calm Trio: Animation Script Directions

Five 120-second vertical (9:16) concepts for AI-generated video. Each card is one scene. The line marked VO / SUBTITLE is both spoken aloud and shown on screen, so it reads clearly with sound off.

Scene codes

Scenes run 1–17 per script. “Scene 17” = the final scene.

Product Insert

Gold field = real product footage composited into the AI scene here.

Climax

Green card = the transformation scene. Give it the most generation time.

Product Hero

Plum card = the hero shot. Cosmetic disclaimer must appear here.

Asterisk *

Any appearance claim marked * carries the cosmetic disclaimer on the end card.

Claim register

US cosmetic rules: describe the look of skin (calmer, more even, radiant). Never treat / cure / reduce inflammation as a condition.

Complete Calm Trio — 3-step system: Reset Soothing Cleanser · Renew Barrier Boosting Serum · Reflect Luminizing Moisturizer. Hero botanical across all scripts: Wild Chrysanthemum (cools heat, soothes the look of redness). Featured trio: Chrysanthemum, Centella, Pearl Powder. Supporting actives by product per Moonbow's ingredient page: Orange Peel / Tremella / Ginseng (Renew), Pearl / Peony / Ginseng (Reflect), Centella / Tea Tree / Green Tea / Licorice / Jujube (Reset).
