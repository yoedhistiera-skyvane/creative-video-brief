NOOCI · Relax + Sleep · Creative Production Brief

Relax + Sleep: Animation Script Directions

Five 120-second vertical (9:16) concepts for AI-generated video. Each card is one scene. The line marked VO / SUBTITLE is both spoken aloud and shown on screen, so it reads clearly with sound off.

Scene codes

Scenes run 1–17 per script. “Scene 17” = the final scene.

Product Insert

Amber field = real product footage composited into the AI scene here.

Climax

Green card = the transformation scene. Give it the most generation time.

Product Hero

Plum card = the hero shot. FDA disclaimer must appear here.

Asterisk *

Any claim marked * carries the FDA structure/function disclaimer on the end card.
